Beyond our Boundaries

This local multiplayer VR game is made for Theater Utrecht - innovationlab, //www.theaterutrecht.nl/innovationlab.

Made in Unreal Engine players are asked to join an experiment where they must decide if the AI in the game has become sentient. The aim of the project was to create the illusion players were facing a real AI. Then doing things with or against the AI that they would later feel regret about doing. Discovering the AI is infact a real person would harbor feelings similar you would feel when you land in Uncanny Valley.

Uncanny Valley was the name of the game. Feeling something is a bit off. This whole creation has been an experiment about that.
