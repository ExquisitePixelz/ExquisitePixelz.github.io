Day by Train

This game ment to reflect on the afterlife. People make mistakes and might live to regret them later. In this short experience you learn to look back on your life and figure out why it wasn't all bad.

each section focuses on a different part of your life, and during all the conducter helps you cope with it all.
Making the game was allot of fun for me. I was targeting quick designing and playtesting, which I did in minecraft. After we decided the layout of the game and what coupe does what. We managed to wizard of oz it in minecraft. We even created some minigames to enhance the feeling of playing the game. Besides getting good feedback before we moved to Unity. We also were able to play with level design.
